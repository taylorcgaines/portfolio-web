Projects

Texas Muck Map

Github Repo

I joined a small team of volunteer developers right after Hurricane Harvey made landfall in 2017. This web app helped to coordinate people who needed rescue from high waters with volunteer rescuers. After most people were rescued, we added the Muck Map feature that helped coordinate people who had homes damaged from the flood with volunteer cleaning teams. Thousands of people were helped that week because of our quickly built little web app!

Technology Stack

HTML/CSS, Javascript, Node, Express, Parse, MongoDB, Google Maps API

My Harvey Needs

Live Site Github Repo

A woman came forward in the days after Harvey who had matched almost 400 people who had lost much after harvey with those who wanted to help, but didn't know how. I joined a team at Slack City to build an app for her to make her work easier. We built an SMS bot into the app for those who had no other access to the service. At last checked, almost 200 people have used the app to ask for assistance!

Technology Stack

HTML/CSS, Javascript, Node, Express, Twilio, Google Sheets, Zapier
